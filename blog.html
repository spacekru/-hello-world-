BUNCE ISLAND

Bounce Island is an Island in Sierra Leone River. It is situated in Free town harbour the estuary of the Rokel River and port loko Creek, about 20 miles upriver from Sierra Leone’s capital city Freetown

What happened on Bounce Island

Between the late sisteenth century and 1807,when it was outlawed by te british government, hundreds of thousands of West Africans become victims of the slave trade,. From BunceIsland, the furthest point upriver that was accessible to commercial ships, slaves were sold to colonies in the West Indies and North America..

Why is Bounc Island so significant

Bounce Island was the largest British slave castle on on the rice coast of West Africa Founded around 1670, it exported tens of thounsand of African captives to north Americaca.

Why is Bounce Island call Bounce Island

Bounce Island was name for captain John Bence a wealthy London marchant and one of the castle’s original investor During it long history, Bounce Island was operated by four London - based companies.
